### DESIGN SPECIFICATION: MATERIAL YOU 3 EXPRESSIVE

#### 1. Core Philosophy & Design Vision
Material 3 Expressive shifts the paradigm from rigid utility to high-fidelity brand personalization, organic dynamics, and deep emotional resonance. It intentionally breaks away from default, boxy Android frames, prioritizing fluid micro-interactions, asymmetric geometry, variable typography, and breathing space.

#### 3. Geometry, Shapes & Hierarchy
Exaggerated shapes and structural asymmetry direct user focus.
* Shape Tokens:
  - `--md-shape-corner-small`: 12px (Chips, context inputs, micro-data badges)
  - `--md-shape-corner-medium`: 16px (Standard content tiles, quick-contact blocks, pop-ups)
  - `--md-shape-corner-large`: 28px (Main feature cards, parent dashboard panels, window states)
  - `--md-shape-corner-full`: 9999px (Pill-shape main CTAs, active indicators, floating background blobs)
* Asymmetric Expressive Accents: For critical tiles or promotional surfaces, implement intentional multi-radius properties (e.g., `border-radius: var(--md-shape-corner-large) var(--md-shape-corner-small) var(--md-shape-corner-large) var(--md-shape-corner-small)`).

#### 4. Typography Matrix
Type acts as an active graphic overlay. Use clean, variable sans-serif choices (e.g., "Plus Jakarta Sans", "Inter") with high tracking and weight differentiation.
* Display / Headlines: Bold, heavy weights (800+ or ExtraBold/Black) with condensed letter-spacing (`letter-spacing: -0.03em`) and crisp, tight line-heights (1.15) to drive impact on metrics and greeting blocks.
* Body / UI Labels: Meticulously balanced, clean weights (400–500) leveraging high text-to-background contrast across both theme states.

#### 5. Motion, Elasticity & Fluid Transitions
Interface components must feel physically alive, snappy, and responsive.
* Physics Easing: All transitions must map to the signature Emphasized curve:
  - `--md-easing-emphasized`: cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0, 1)
  - `--md-duration-transition`: 0.5s
* Signature Micro-interactions:
  - Ambient Fluidity: Background layers use scale-shifting floating blobs animating infinitely with relaxed timing loops (~25s) to establish soft environmental lighting in both light and dark token spaces.
  - Interactive Blob Morphing: Image containers and premium controls use fluctuating border-radiuses with continuous fluid shifts (`@keyframes shapeMorph`).
  - Magnetic Hover Interactivity: Interactive focus points or avatar areas must hook into precise cursor tracking transformations, shifting up to 25% towards mouse positioning on entry and gently auto-snapping back on drop.
